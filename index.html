Here Is Your Nigiri Sushi Cheat Sheet


This web tool is designed for who wants to learn some fundamental knowledges of nigiri sushis in playful and interactive way. By simply drag and drop a sushi from the left side to the sushi stand, you can find out the name of that sushi in English and Japanese. To know more about nigiri sushi, I encourage you to visit The Sushi Geek.
